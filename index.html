About Me
I am a recent graduate in Bachelor of Computing with a major in Software Engineering at the University of Guelph. Before university I was a cell phone technician, and part of my job was to also handle the software related part of the phones with unlocking or fixing them. I quickly realized I loved software more than I did working with the hardware. A few months later I joined University of Guelph and I fell in love with programming, software development, agile software development, and most importantly always challenging my brain by always learning new software, new methodologies and new software languages. Its been a wonderful 4 years at the university, and I am excited to continue my career on this path.
